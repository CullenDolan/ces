Solutions and Services

Problem Definition and Requirements Development

All technical projects require a structured process for understanding the business problem and requirements.

  • Understand and map out current state of the business and technical processes
  • Define business requirements and technical requirements
  • Determine if there are non-technical solutions to solve the business needs
  • Present a high level Software Architecture

Detailed Design

If this software architecture meets your needs we would move into the detailed design phase

  • Detailed analysis of current IT infrastructure
  • Understand data requirements and privacy needs
  • Review exsisting security policies
  • Define unit, integration and system testing
  • Review existing reporting capabilities ur future reporting needs

Implementation

You can take this plan and use your internal team or another team to implement the solution, or we can implement

  • Build cloud infrastructure defined in the plan
  • Integrate necessary applications
  • Build reports or dashboards
  • Train team members on applications or services

Monitoring and Maintenance

Cloud services take care of most of the work to keep IT infrasturcure running and secure, but there is some maintenance required. We can train your team on the cloud platform or manage it for you.

  • Training on the cloud service
  • Review maintenance plan and service level agreement
